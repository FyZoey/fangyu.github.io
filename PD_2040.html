Mobility in the Last One Mile

  • Life in Urban Space 
    Re-urbanization is happening in America, people would like to live in the center of a city in the future, per capita living space will decrease while the need of living functions won‚Äôt, so those services and activities that improve life quality but less frequently be used will move towards cities rather than to families.
  • Public Sharing Transportation 
    5G is coming, IoT will be a common service in the future, let alone autonomous automobiles, while people will hardly have motivations to purchase private autonomous vehicles since no driving experience is needed. But people still need private space, so public sharing transportation system that provides relatively private space will be more possible in the future.
  • Better Mobility 
    Lighter material, smaller and more efficient batteries will be available on automobiles in the future, people would get better mobility and can be taken to even the narrowest road in the city.
  • Focused Activities: Working and Studying 
    Vehicle space is quite suitable for group working / studying scenario, especially for a future with a trend of working at home, the space is private, time promised and make you concentrated. Plus, sharing cars will enable people who don‚Äôt know each others to meet, they all have higher education, the vehicle space is just suitable for sharing experiences and knowledges.

User & Scenario

v

Users and scenarios are set before thinking about design concept, though finally it turned out that it could be an universal solution. 4 Groups of people will be worth paying attention:

  • Elderlies 
    There will be more old people, more than 1 in 5 Americans will be over the age of 65. Elderly people will be augmented to gain better movability. They have enough experiences and love to share with people to reduce loneliness.
  • Minorities 
    Minorities will become the majority in the U.S. by 2045. They work for the U.S,, numerous of experiences and knowledges are stored in their own minds.
  • Freelancers 
    More freelancers work at any spot they like because of development of distance work technologies. Even though they can get connected with internet, freelancers are still lonely if only work on their own.
  • Students 
    Because of development of communication technology, students don‚Äôt have to travel for learning basic knowledges, but traveling around still provides good opportunities for learning personal experiences from others.

Initial Concept

To provide service for future working and studying scenarios with transportation, the concept is initially ‚Äòimproves the efficiency of your time with a private place that enables better concentration, eliminate the waiting time for finding a sharing vehicle, the traveling time on the road to a working place, and the time you wait for the people you are going to meet‚Äô.

Final Concept

The concept is basically a ‚Äòsharing wheel system‚Äô. By ordering a meeting with people, transportation system automatically send you a wheel to pick you up to a pod, then your wheel will joint a pod, so that the pod with 4 sharing wheels can become a sharing car to take you further distance. 

Most of the sharing wheels and all of the pods are parked in main stations, which are represented by larger dots on the map, so that the main station can be not only a charging place for vehicles, but also a meeting place for group of people. The wheels can also be charged at any other secondary stations (smaller dots on the map) distributed locally, so that the wheel it self can provide mobilities in neighborhood.
